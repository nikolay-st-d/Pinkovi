ABOUT THE PINKOVI HOTEL

Family Hotel PINKOVI is nestled in a vibrant and rapidly growing area of Nessebar, parallel to the beautiful beach coast. Its convenient location provides easy access to the main road connecting the popular tourist resorts of Nessebar and Ravda.

It boasts a prime location right by the slopes of a serene Black Sea beach, offering breathtaking panoramic views of the coastline from each of its 20 rooms.

Despite the growing interest in the region, Family Hotel PINKOVI offers a peaceful and serene environment, providing everything you need for a fantastic beach vacation.

BISTRO

Bistro PINKOVI, situated on the hotel's ground floor, offers both outdoor and indoor seating.

It provides a cozy and serene setting for enjoying a cup of coffee, lunch, or dinner with friends.

Our menu features a selection of Bulgarian and European cuisine, with a focus on seafood dishes. While our main fish menu predominantly showcases Black Sea delicacies, we also serve fish from Greece, Turkey, Italy, France, and Norway.

ENTERTAINMENT

Family Hotel PINKOVI is nestled along the picturesque and serene coastline of a beautiful bay. The beach provides a lifeguard, as well as parasols and lounges for your comfort. Additionally, you can enjoy a range of thrilling water activities such as banana boating, water wheels, water skiing, water surfing, wind surfing, and even take part in fitness and surfing lessons. When the sun sets, Nessebar comes alive with its vibrant nightlife, offering a plethora of clubs, restaurants, and bars to explore.

We also provide tourist information for various exciting events, tours, and excursions, as well as convenient options for car rentals, bicycle hires, and taxi services.

LOCATION AND TRANSPORT

BULGARIA, 8230 Nessebar, 24 “Aurelia” district, Family hotel PINKOVI

Family Hotel PINKOVI is conveniently situated just 1.8km from Ravda, 1.5km south of Nessebar, and only 5km from Sunny Beach. Adjacent to the hotel, you'll find a bus stop on the main road that connects Ravda and Nessebar, offering swift transportation to Bourgas, Nessebar, Sunny Beach, and Pomorie.

Bourgas Airport is approximately 15km away from the hotel.
